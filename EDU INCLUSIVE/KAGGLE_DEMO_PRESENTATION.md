## Slide 1: The Silent Exclusion (0:00 - 0:20)
### 🎨 Visual Guidance
* **Slide Elements**: Bold text reading *"Over 34 million children globally suffer from disabling hearing loss"* paired with a clean minimalist icon of a muted bell.
* **On-Screen Subtitles**: *How do we ensure no child is left behind in the modern IT revolution?*

### 🎙️ Speech Script
> "Imagine entering a classroom where the teacher speaks, video materials play, and your classmates collaborate—but you hear absolutely nothing. 
> Over 34 million children worldwide struggle with disabling hearing loss. Standard classroom setups and blended learning materials are built primarily for verbal learners, silently excluding these kids from crucial technical fields like Computer Science. 
> It's time to bridge this gap."

> Powered by Google's state-of-the-art **Gemini AI**, we have engineered a single-screen learning platform that pairs visual, text-based, and kinetic sign-language tools.

## 💡 Pro-Tips for a Winning Kaggle Presentation
1. **Pace Yourself**: 2 minutes passes quickly. Practice speaking clearly, confidently, and with moderate pauses to allow slides to sync.
2. **Focus on the 'Why'**: Judges love technical implementations, but they remember the human-centric impact. Highlight the "ASL & IT Mode" as a major accessibility triumph.
3. **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Keep the slide visuals dynamic. Let the charts and drawing board actions speak for themselves!
